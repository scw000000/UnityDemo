Baseline requirement:

   move left/right by pressing a/d
   move up/down by pressing w/s
   move forward/backward by pressing q/e
   Go back to the center point by pressing r

Extra features:

   cube rotation:
      rotate around X axis by pressing y/h
      rotate around Y axis by pressing g/j
      rotate around Z axis by pressing t/u

   cube scaling:
      scale up by pressing =
      scale down by pressing -

   camera control:
      A conventional 3rd person camera is implemented, and it will follow the movement of the cube.
      However, when the cube is rotating, the rotation of the camera will stay the same.
      Rotate the camera by holding left mouse button and moving the mouse.
